Scar Treatment


My goal is to help people feel more confident about themselves and their skin by safely and effectively treating skin damage. I work on scars that leave behind a harsh traumatic remembrance or simply that are visually undesirable.

Micro-pigmentation is the process of depositing microns of colored pigment into the dermal layer of the skin. Micro-pigmentation is a complex pigmentation procedure often requiring several applications and techniques to create blending and natural appearance. This procedure is sought out by those (including both men and women) who wish to camouflage their scars by creating an even skin tone.

Tattoo Removal


Saline/Salabrasion Tattoo Removal is a more natural yet effective alternative to laser tattoo removal. The cost of Saline/Salabrasion tattoo removal is often less expensive and less traumatic to the skin than laser, dermabrasion, and excision. Microblading Basic Course
Our comprehensive Microblading Basic Course has grown along with the expanding Permanent Cosmetics Industry to incorporate the constant flow of new information and developments. We make certain that students are receiving the training they need in order to safely perform their procedures. At first comprised of three sessions, my Microblading Basic Course expanded to five days - and on January 1, 2009 a sixth day of class was added to include instruction on Blood-borne Pathogens and Exposure control, ensuring that graduates are well-rounded and thoroughly informed on safety issues.
