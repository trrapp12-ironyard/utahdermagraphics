Scar Treatment


My goal is to help people feel more confident about themselves and their skin by safely and effectively treating skin damage. I work on scars that leave behind a harsh traumatic remembrance or simply that are visually undesirable.

Micro-pigmentation is the process of depositing microns of colored pigment into the dermal layer of the skin. Micro-pigmentation is a complex pigmentation procedure often requiring several applications and techniques to create blending and natural appearance. This procedure is sought out by those (including both men and women) who wish to camouflage their scars by creating an even skin tone.

Tattoo Removal


Saline/Salabrasion Tattoo Removal is a more natural yet effective alternative to laser tattoo removal. The cost of Saline/Salabrasion tattoo removal is often less expensive and less traumatic to the skin than laser, dermabrasion, and excision. This new technique is also much less painful the traditional laser or other methods.
